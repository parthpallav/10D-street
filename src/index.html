Franchise
Success Stories

Chennai, 2006

Faced initial operational hiccups but stabilized with 10D's hands-on support, expert guidance in operations and marketing turned it around. Now a go-to nightlife spot, showcasing the franchisor's ability to rescue and elevate struggling outlets.

Bhopal, 2011

Launched on June 16, 2011, as Bhopal's first elite pub, kickstarting the city's nightlife culture. Broke even in just 2.5 years, ahead of the 3–4 year projection, and has thrived for 14 years—famous among youngsters for its vibe and offerings like "Pub Lunch."

Kolkata, 2016

Launched in 2016, bringing the iconic pub culture experience to one of India's most vibrant metropolitan cities. Quickly emerged as a preferred nightlife destination with energetic weekends, strong repeat clientele, and a well-balanced food, music, and hospitality experience. Over the years, the outlet has built a loyal customer base and continues to thrive as a landmark social destination in Kolkata.

Coimbatore, 2017

Opened in 2017, introducing a premium pub experience to Coimbatore's rapidly growing lifestyle market. Backed by consistent operational support, curated entertainment, and a strong food & beverage mix, the outlet established itself as a leading nightlife and dining destination. Today, it stands as a strong example of sustainable growth and successful franchise performance in an emerging market.
